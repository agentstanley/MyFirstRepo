Brief Introduction About Myself
Stanley Wahoro is a driven and ambitious young innovator from Kenya. A 2025 beVisioneers: Mercedes-Benz Fellow and ALX Foundation Pathway scholar, he is passionate about using technology and entrepreneurship to solve real-world problems. Coming from a background shaped by resilience and determination, Stanley is focused on building solutions that make a positive impact, particularly in underserved communities. He is currently exploring solar energy, software development, and digital education as tools for sustainable change, and continues to grow as a leader committed to lifelong learning and social impact.
